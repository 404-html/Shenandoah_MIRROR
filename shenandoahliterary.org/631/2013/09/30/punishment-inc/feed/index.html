]]> By: Beverly https://shenandoahliterary.org/631/2013/09/30/punishment-inc/#comment-2941 Sat, 22 Mar 2014 15:46:06 +0000 http://shenandoahliterary.org/631/?p=265#comment-2941 Three things made this story stand out to me.
1. The constant return of the wayward husband to take one more stupid thing from the wife he left makes the reader dread every day and what more he can do to her

2. The fresh take on revenge, with the breezy and imaginative packages the Punishment Company offers
3. The use of ice cream as a narrative knife.

Endings are always hard, but the author managed to add another layer by having the main character abruptly and very vividly describe both Daryl’s new look and the impact of that kiss. Well done, and a lot of fun.
