The ending is wonderful, and I particularly like the lack of punctuation after “this,” because it adds a feeling of trailing off that goes with the natural flow of the poem. The poem gives a list but also sounds conversational, much like someone would sound talking to someone in fear. I think that this intimate yet conversational feel is the poem’s biggest strengths: each “descriptor” of a shadow could stand alone or unite without the ending, and the poem would still be beautiful and haunting.
