How each of us well knows, “all appetite and sound.” Yet how else could we be?–and what else could he do save trade the horses: a deal he bargained for as, redemptively, he must have (I pray!) wanted the “ours,” the “we’s,” or at least something anyway in that initial quid pro quo.
