Latella https://shenandoahliterary.org/652/2016/03/15/the-bad-poetry-reading-kirby/#comment-2 Tue, 05 Apr 2016 20:07:39 +0000 http://shenandoahliterary.org/652/?p=148#comment-2 This poem was entertaining from start to finish. I think what I enjoy most is the honest tone that is woven throughout the entire piece. Every writer has had that experience of feeling like the piece they’ve chosen to share at a public reading is “bad;” you get up to the podium, and there’s no turning back. No matter how “bad” you think your poem or short story is, you’ve committed yourself to reading it. I think Mr. Kirby has done a great job of capturing these feelings of self doubt by putting a humorous spin on this nerve-wracking experience. In the end, he explains that a poetry reading has the power to inspire audience members and to pull people out of a dark place, which is motivation enough for writers everywhere to stand up and read their “bad” poems. Even if you think it’s bad, someone out there will think otherwise. And your words may just save that person’s life.
