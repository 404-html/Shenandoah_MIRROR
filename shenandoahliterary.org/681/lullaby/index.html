Lullaby

Mita Mahato

Mita Mahato is a Seattle-based cut paper, collage, and comics artist. Her poetry comics are collected in In Between (Pleiades, 2017) and her cut paper art has been exhibited in galleries across the country. She is an associate professor of English at the University of Puget Sound, serves on the board for the arts organization Short Run Seattle, and is a teaching artist with the Henry Art Gallery.
