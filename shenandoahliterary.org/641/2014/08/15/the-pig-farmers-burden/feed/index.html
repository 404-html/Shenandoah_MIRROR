]]> By: Stephanie Rice https://shenandoahliterary.org/641/2014/08/15/the-pig-farmers-burden/#comment-465 Mon, 01 Dec 2014 21:52:58 +0000 http://shenandoahliterary.org/641/?p=44#comment-465 This is a story that kept me reading. When I began it, I had no clear intention of finishing; I just wanted to pass a few minutes But it hooked me, and I just had to keep going. I truly enjoyed reading this story, and it’s even more enjoyable a second time, now that I can spot some of the subtle moments of foreshadowing. The written-out dialect, so often obnoxious, works well, and I found myself surprised at how much I liked the voice. Overall, the language, the words used and the way they’re put together is exceptional. The pacing is very effective as well, slowly laying the groundwork before the rapid successive revelations at the end. But aside from the literary structure, I had real emotional engagement with the story. By the end I felt slightly stunned and more than a little delighted – my main thought simply, “What a good story.”
