There’s such distinctly different characterization between Henry and Beth – Henry blunt and strong and willing to risk, Beth more held back, cautious – and so much of it captured and conveyed in the details, the little things (Henry needing Beth to stop her from biting her own cheek, it was Henry who suggested the ice boat).
